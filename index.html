Charles Smith

Curriculum Developer and Facilitator

Charles received his BA in Liberal Arts and Science/Mathematics with a concentration in Secondary Education from Ashford University in Iowa. He also has a Master’s of Science specializing in Brain-based Teaching and Learning from Nova Southeastern University in Florida. Operations Manager

Alex Lee

Tenissa Smith

Customer Service Manager

Tenissa works directly with the parents of the campers attending SuperCamp. She trains Site Administrators and Site Counselors on camper pertinent information systems, as well as customer service techniques. Her team keeps us up to date on camper information and needs. Tenissa joined QLN in 2009 and has since earned both a Bachelor’s degree in Sociology and Master’s degree in Psychology. She enjoys spending time with family, writing, and being outdoors.

Jeff Miller

Academic Advisor/Consultant

Jeff collaborates with the Education Team to ensure quality curriculum and programming company wide, and is responsible for the professional development and training of Facilitators. Jeff joined QLN in 2012 and has worked as a Facilitator and Lead Facilitator. Jeff graduated with a Bachelor’s of Arts with a double major in Mass Communications and Theatre and later obtained a Master of Fine Arts in Creative Writing. Prior to joining Quantum Learning Network, Jeff spent ten years as a classroom teacher and a theater director in Baltimore, MD and Colorado Springs, CO. In his spare time he enjoys golfing, writing, and creating live theatre.
