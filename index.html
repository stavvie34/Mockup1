GET TO KNOW OUR SUPERCAMP TEAM

Each and every staff member at SuperCamp, from those at our headquarters in Oceanside, CA to our Facilitators around the world, want the best for your child.

Get to know us!

We've got what you, and your child need!

With award-winning, dedicated, and highly qualified staff memebers, your SuperCamp experience is sure to be astounding. Many of our staff members are previous campers who were so inspired by their experience, they have decided to dedicate themselves to sharing the same lessons with others!

How about training?

Our Training

4:1 Ratio

Our student to staff ratio enables us to give your child the attention they deserve and require for success.

Extensive Training

Our facilitators participate in more than 150 hours of extensive training in the proprietary Quantum Learning system.

1000+ Applications/Year

We are able to hand pick the best candidates to ensure your child is constantly surrounded by positive, encouraging role models.

Rigorous Selection Proccess

Only the most qualified, committed, and caring candidates are chosen to be part of the SuperCamp team.
